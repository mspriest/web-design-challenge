Latitude vs. Max Temperature(F)

Based on the scatter plot above, a city's maximum temperature(F) correlates with its latitude. The weather becomes significantly warmer the closer the city is to the equator (0 degrees latitude). The Southern Hemisphere tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere at the time this data was captured.
